Orgyen Dorje Den, 2244 Santa Clara Ave Alameda, CA 94501
On the 1st, 2nd and 4th Mondays of every month unless otherwise noted.
Please check the ODD calendar to confirm dates.
Lama Drimed will give brief teachings on the development of compassion and lead guided meditations including the recitation of the six syllable mantra, Om Mani Padme Hung. This mantra is considered to be the essence of compassion and is the most ubiquitous in Tibetan Buddhism. New students, experienced practitioners and people just curious about meditation are all encouraged to attend.
