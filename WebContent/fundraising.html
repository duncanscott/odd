Help Raise a Troma Thangka at ODD

Ven. Gyatrul Rinpoche and Sangye Khandro encourage the establishment of Troma Nagmo practice at ODD. To assist in this aspiration, ODD's Troma Practice Group is commissioning a Troma thangka for the shrine room depicting the full mandala of dakinis. The thangka will support the ongoing practice of chod at the center.

Many thanks for your donations. The fundraising goal for this project has been met.
