Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What are the dates of the Rinchen Terzod empowerments?
    November 7th, 2010 thru February, 2011

  2. What is the exact end date?
    Not yet determined. We will let you know once we have that information. Registering is the best way to stay up to date with the details of the program.
  3. Do we know the daily schedule?
     The daily schedule is not yet determined, but will likely be one long session starting each afternoon.

  4. Can anyone attend?
     All students, new and experienced, are welcome to attend these transmissions. The prerequisite is a good motivation and the aspiration to achieve enlightenment!

  5. Will the program be everyday?
    The exact schedule is not yet determined, however, considering the scope, we think Yangthang Rinpoche will bestow empowerments every day. If there are scheduled breaks, we will let you know and post it on the website. Check the Wangs page often for updates

  6. Can I attend a day at a time?
     Of course attending the whole program is best, but we can attend on a daily basis according to our available time. However, there may be some empowerments that take place over 2 days, in which case, if you miss the first day, you won't be able to attend the second. When these arise, we will try to inform everyone and post it on the website. Check back often for updates.

  7. Do you know the sequence of empowerments and the dates each will be given?
     We don't have that information yet, however, the first few days will be focused on Vajrasattva, then about 25% on Lama, then 25% Yidam, then 25% Khandro, then 25% Dharmapalas. To the extent we can establish the sequence of empowerments prior to the start of the wangchen we will let you know. Registration is the best way to ensure you receive updates via email. For updates, check the Wangs page often..

  8. Will Yangthang Rinpoche give the lung?
    At this point we do not think there will be enough time for the bestowal of the lung, the reading transmission.

  9. How do I register?
    Download and print out the PDF form. Complete it mail it to:
                    Orgyen Dorje Den

            2244 Santa Clara Avenue

            Alameda, CA 94501

  1. What is the suggested donation?
     $2300 for the entire program or $30 per day.

  2. Can I pay daily at the door?
     Yes. Checks, Cash or Visa and Mastercard. Please register in advance though, as seating may be limited.

  3. Are any work study situations available?
    Some work study opportunities will be available on a case by case basis.

  4. Will food be provided during the 3 month program?
    Food will not be available at ODD, but there are many excellent restaurants and coffee shops within 2-3 blocks of the temple.

  5. Can attendees offer help during the wangs?
     Absolutely! We will need a lot of help and support during the program, so please plan to participate as much as you can! Soon we will post more details about our various needs and ways in which you can help.
  6. Can I help sponsor the event?
    Yes! As a means of accumulating merit, some people are able to be sponsors and underwrite some or all of the considerable costs associated with hosting Dharma events of this type; some people have already expressed interest in contributing in this way. Any donation, large or small, will surely establish a connection for you with Ven. Yangthang Tulku Rinpoche and this profound mandala. Please see our Support page for details.
  1. Is housing available at ODD?
     We do not have any accommodations available at Orgyen Dorje Den at this time. We have negotiated a special rate at the Coral Reef Inn. This independent motel is conveniently located within walking distance to ODD and our sangha members have stayed there comfortably for many years.  Please contact them directly via phone or email if you would like to make a reservation or for more information.
    Click here for more details. 
  2. Is parking available?
     Parking is not available at ODD. Street parking is allowed and there is a low cost pay lot next to CVS, one block away.
