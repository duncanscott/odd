Evening Sitting Meditation
Orgyen Dorje Den, 2244 Santa Clara Ave Alameda, CA 94501
Every other Wednesday (unless otherwise noted) 7-9pm
See the calendar for schedule. Sign up for the email list to receive confimation and change notices.
Les Collins, a long time student of Gyatrul Rinpoche, conducts a sitting meditation practice group every other Wednesday Evening at 7pm. The program is open to all and includes practice and instruction based on the text taught by Gyatrul Rinpoche called Natural Liberation.
Guru Rinpoche Days & Dakini Days (Tsog)
Orgyen Dorje Den, 2244 Santa Clara Ave Alameda, CA 94501
See the calendar for scheduled tsog practice days and times.
On the 10th and 25th days of the Lunar calendar, the sangha gathers to make offerings and renew our samaya commitments through meditation on Guru Rinpoche and the Dakini Yeshe Tsogyal. All are welcome to join in these practices from the Dudjom Tersar tradition. Please bring food or drink to share.
