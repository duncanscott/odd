Orgyen Dorje Den - Matching Funds Campaign

Right now, we have a generous sponsor who is offering to match donations dedicated to paying off ODD's remaining mortgage--allowing our gifts to double!

Gyatrul Rinpoche has said that he has aspirations for ODD to benefit beings for a thousand years, and tells us to "work together in harmony to enrich ourselves through hearing, contemplation and meditation" and to "create conducive circumstances for the authentic transmission of the dharma" for ourselves and future generations.

At this moment, with this unprecedented opportunity before us, we are asking everyone who has been inspired by Ven. Gyatrul Rinpoche and the many great masters who have taught at ODD to contribute by Losar 2012. Your offering will be used for the retirement of the mortgage and will help ensure the longevity of ODD.

Please make a donation, or pledge your support today...but don't stop there! Encourage your sangha friends and family to join in this effort.
